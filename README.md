# 🛡️ Earnsure

### 🚀 Problem Statement

Quick commerce delivery partners (Zepto, Blinkit, etc.) frequently lose income due to **uncontrollable external disruptions** such as extreme weather, warehouse shutdowns, and platform outages.

These disruptions can reduce their working hours by 20–30%, with **no financial safety net** currently available.

---

### 🎯 Our Solution

**Earnsure** is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform that provides **automatic income protection** for delivery workers.

- Detects disruptions in real-time  
- Calculates actual income loss  
- Automatically triggers payouts  

> 💡 We insure **lost earning time**, not just events.

---

### 👤 Persona & Workflow

#### Scenario 1: Heavy Rain  
Ravi usually works 8 hours/day. Due to heavy rain, he works only 5 hours.

- Lost hours detected  
- Income loss calculated  
- Automatic payout credited  

#### Scenario 2: Warehouse Failure  
- Dark store shuts down  
- Workers active but no orders  
- System detects anomaly → payout triggered  

#### Scenario 3: Platform Downtime  
- Orders drop to zero  
- Workers idle  
- Automatic compensation  

---

#### Workflow

    1. User registers and activates weekly coverage  
    2. System detects active delivery zone dynamically  
    3. AI calculates risk score  
    4. Weekly premium generated  
    5. Real-time monitoring of disruptions  
    6. Automatic payout on valid triggers  

---

### 💰 Weekly Insurance Model

#### 🎚️ Coverage-Based Pricing

Users select coverage level:
- 50% to 90% of income  

---

#### 💡 Premium Formula
Premium = Base Price + Risk Factor + Coverage Factor


- Base Price = ₹20/week  
- Risk Factor = Zone + Weather + History  
- Coverage Factor = Selected coverage %

---

#### 📊 Example

| Coverage | Risk | Premium |
|---------|------|--------|
| 50%     | Low  | ₹18    |
| 70%     | Medium | ₹30 |
| 90%     | High | ₹45   |

---

#### 💸 Payout Model
Payout = Lost Hours × Hourly Income × Coverage %


Example:  
₹100/hour × 3 hours × 70% = ₹210 payout
